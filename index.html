The Challenge

CCC Intelligent Solutions needed to unify project management across 150+ users in sales, marketing, and product teams. The organization was struggling with disparate tools, poor cross-functional visibility, and inefficient collaboration workflows that were hindering productivity and causing project delays.

Solution Approach

As the Solutions Consultant & Implementation Lead, I conducted comprehensive stakeholder analysis across all departments to understand existing workflows and pain points. I designed a custom workspace architecture with department-specific templates while maintaining cross-functional visibility, developed a phased rollout strategy starting with pilot groups to ensure adoption success, and created a change management program with a champion network for peer-to-peer support and training.

Implementation Process

  • Discovery (2 wks): 25+ stakeholder interviews, current state workflow mapping.
  • Design (3 wks): Custom template creation, integration planning, pilot group selection.
  • Pilot (4 wks): 20-user pilot across 3 high-visibility projects with feedback loops.
  • Rollout (8 wks): Department-by-department implementation with training and support.

Business Outcomes

  • Productivity Improvement: >25% increase in project completion velocity.
  • User Adoption: 90%+ sustained adoption across all departments (industry benchmark: 60-70%).
  • Operational Efficiency: 40% reduction in project delays through improved visibility.
  • Executive Impact: 8 hours per week saved in status meetings due to real-time transparency.
  • ROI Achievement: 6-month payback period, 300%+ ROI in first year.
