Case Studies

Automated GTM Strategy Workflow

The Challenge: A rapidly growing tech firm was struggling with a manual, inefficient, and error-prone process for developing go-to-market strategies for new products, leading to significant delays and misaligned launches.

My Solution: I designed and built an internal web application that automated the entire GTM workflow. The tool guides product managers through a step-by-step process, from market analysis to launch planning, using a series of structured inputs and generating a cohesive strategy document. This eliminated redundant data entry, enforced a consistent strategic framework, and provided leadership with a real-time dashboard of all upcoming launches.

The Impact: The solution reduced the GTM planning cycle by 40%, decreased strategy errors by 95%, and provided a scalable platform that now supports the company's entire product portfolio.

Sales Enablement Content Hub

The Challenge: The global sales team lacked a centralized, easy-to-access repository for critical sales collateral, such as case studies, data sheets, and competitive battle cards. This resulted in wasted time, inconsistent messaging, and lost sales opportunities.

My Solution: I developed a dynamic, searchable content hub that served as the single source of truth for the entire sales organization. The system categorized all assets by product, industry, and sales stage, and included a robust search function. I integrated it with their existing CRM to provide contextual content recommendations directly within the sales workflow.

The Impact: Increased the sales team's efficiency by an estimated 15%, improved message consistency across all regions, and was credited with helping to close several key deals by providing the right information at the right time.
