Asana Enterprise Implementation

Unified six GTM portfolios; onboarded 150+ users; increased GTM velocity 25% and saved executives 8 hrs/wk.

The Challenge

Disparate tools and no standard playbook across six product portfolios created low visibility and slow launches.

Solution

Designed product-specific templates, custom fields, rules/approvals, and a phased rollout with a champion network.

Implementation

  • Discovery (2 wks): Stakeholder interviews; current-state mapping.
  • Design (3 wks): Templates; integration plan; pilot selection.
  • Pilot (4 wks): 20-user pilot; feedback loops.
  • Rollout (8 wks): Portfolio-by-portfolio enablement; live training; office hours.

Outcomes

  • 25% faster GTM completion velocity.
  • 90%+ sustained adoption across Sales, Product, CS, Legal.
  • 8 hrs/wk executive time saved; fewer status meetings.
  • 65% fewer GTM emails; ~45 minutes saved per user/day.
